THE LENDROID COMMUNITY

The
Lender

A person who is sitting on a “stable” liquid digital asset (ETH, DGX) on which he is looking to earn a risk free profit.

The
Borrower

A person who is sitting on a digital asset which he is not willing to sell, but is looking for some short term liquidity.

The Lendroid Support
Token holder (LST)

Token holders can choose to create, moderate, and underwrite loan markets.

explore lendroid

A marketplace that enables borrowers to avail instant low-cost digital asset loans and lenders to earn interest on digital assets they lend, all on-chain!

Lendroid Support Token

These tokens are locked in risk pools by guarantors as a way of guiding lenders to those loans they believe will be ideal for both the lenders and the borrowers.

The LST holders (aka guarantors) study the current market fluctuations, and invest in those risk pools which they believe will thrive.

The risk pools the borrowers are exposed to depend on the type and level of collateral they wish to pledge.

Multiple risk pools are then created. The borrower chooses the risk pool that has the highest number of LSTs and lowest interest rate.

In the above example, the borrower opts for risk pool 1. The deciding factor being the interest rate, when compared to the total loanable amount and the collateral level displayed.

lendroid creates

A shared liquidity
pool for crypto

1

When Lendroid is newly established, like any other new market, the liquidity is bound to be low. Therefore not enough lenders are motivated to participate and lend.

2

The Lendroid Support Token holders will then make an effort to spread the word across other communities to attract lenders.

3

As lenders begin to entrust in Lendroid, the number of borrowers also increase. With an increase in lenders, their options also widen.

4

In addition to borrowers benefiting from lower interest rates, the entire Lendroid community reaps the benefit of higher loan quantities as a result of increased number of participants.

Example markets

We have decided to work with ENS as our first digital asset. Domain names issued by ENS make for great collaterals, because there exists a deposit behind each of these unique domain names, and this deposit makes the name at least as valuable as the deposit. Therefore, borrowers can pledge their domain names and borrow loans against them. ENS, aside from ERC20 tokens, has helped shape Lendroid for us and is therefore of great importance.
